Early Detection Saves Lives

Breast cancer is treatable, especially when detected early. Learn about symptoms, risk factors, and preventive actions every woman should know...

What is Breast Cancer?

Breast cancer occurs when abnormal cells in the breast begin to grow uncontrollably. It typically starts in the ducts (which carry milk) or lobules (milk-producing glands)...

What Causes Breast Cancer?

Several factors increase the risk: genetic mutations (BRCA1/2), age, hormonal changes, family history, lifestyle habits, etc.

Stages of Breast Cancer

Stage 0: Non-invasive
Stage I-II: Localized
Stage III: Spread to tissues
Stage IV: Metastatic

Can It Be Prevented?

Maintain a healthy lifestyle, get regular screenings, avoid smoking, exercise, and limit alcohol to reduce the risk.
